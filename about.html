About

Fate/Grand Order is a gacha game based off of the Type-Moon franchise (primarily Fate) incorporating a number of characters from each of its works such as Fate/Stay Night, Fate/Extra, Fate/Samurai Remnant, Fate/Apocrypha, and only recently characters from Tsukihime, their debut title that released waaayyy back in 2000. F/GO boasts having an incredible narrative both written in part and directed by Kinoku Nasu, the author of visual novel giants like Fate/Stay Night, Tsukihime, and Witch on the Holy Night, blowing away reader expectations with each new main story update.

I first started playing the game back in 2019 when I was 13, and still play it to this day when I am 20. It prompted me to read and play the afforementioned games, and only drew me in further with its incredible world building and writing. I hope that you will read more about the servants featured on this website, because each of them have their own story to tell. Thank you for visiting Chaldeas.
